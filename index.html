Welcome to my blog. I study master of computer engineering at Iran University of Science and Technology (IUST). I have got my B.Sc. in computer engineering/ software from Arak University . Here I share some of my academic works and projects with you.

My research interest is about Automate Software Engineering (ASE), specially automate software testing. Currently, I has focused on fuzz testing and test data generation in file format fuzzers. I want to use deep neural networks to statistically capture the format of highly complex file structures and then build a generative model to generate new test data. It seems that there is a straight relationship between well-formed files and percentage of code coverage when Software Under Test (SUT) was executed with such files as input.

I am a member of IUST Reverse Engineering Laboratory and work under the supervision of Dr. Saeed Parsa. You can find and read more information here .

From summer 2013 my friends and I also maintain Micropedia a free software engineering/ programming tutorials website in persian. You can find some basic programming tutorials on Micropedia.
